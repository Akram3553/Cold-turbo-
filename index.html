Car Information

Toyota Supra MK4

The Toyota Supra MK4, also known as the A80, is one of the most iconic sports cars ever made. It was produced from 1993 to 2002 and is famous for its 2JZ-GTE inline-six engine, which is highly tunable and capable of producing enormous horsepower. The MK4 Supra became a legend due to its appearance in the Fast and Furious franchise and its enormous potential in the tuning community.

Nissan GTR R34

The Nissan GTR R34, produced between 1999 and 2002, is another legendary Japanese sports car. It features the RB26DETT inline-six engine, all-wheel drive, and advanced technology for its time, such as the ATTESA E-TS Pro system. The R34 is revered for its performance and has a massive fanbase, partly due to its prominent role in video games and movies like the Fast and Furious series.
